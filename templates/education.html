Description

One more major issue which is troubling our nation is proper education. The first thing that strikes in our minds when we think about education is gaining knowledge. Education expands vision and outlook to see the world. Education helps in removing poverty as if a person is educated, he can get a good job and fulfill all the basic needs and requirements of his family. One of the most important benefits of education is that it improves personal lives and helps the society to run smoothly. By providing education, poverty can be removed and every person can provide their contribution in developing the country. Majority of the students are not able to attend school due to poverty and other reasons. These children leave school before they acquire the basic skills and knowledge needed to thrive within the society. Our foundation will support and address this problem by providing school books, uniforms and other essentials to the students who cannot afford them and access the best possible education.
